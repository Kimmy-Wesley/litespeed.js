Features

  • MVC

    Simple Model-View-Controller implementation to help separate your business logic from view logic

  • Binding

    Simple 2 way binding between your services and user input using native Object.observe for best performance

  • Routing

    Simple routing system to route users between different states in your app

  • Services

    Simple service dependency handling. No providers and no factories - just a plain services
